Short Bio

I am now a lecturer at Shanghai University. I got my Ph.D. from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2014 and was a Research Fellow at Singapore Nanyang Technological University in 2015.

My research interests include: Network Security, Trust Management, Vulnerability Detection and Machine Learning.
