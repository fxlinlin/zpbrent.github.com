Short Bio

I am now a lecturer at Shanghai University. I got my Ph.D. from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2014 and was a Research Fellow at Singapore Nayang Technological University in 2015.

My researach interests include, but not limited to: Vulnerability Detection and Mining, Intelligient Security System, Machine Learning, Trust Management, Network Measurement Security.
